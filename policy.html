Privacy Policy – Kufar Cookie Manager

Effective date: December 2025

Kufar Cookie Manager ("we", "our", or "the Extension") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use the Extension.

All data is stored locally in your browser; we do not process or transfer it elsewhere.
